ML Hackathon 2025

Event Details

Date: March 27-28, 2025

Location: AI Innovation Lab

Description: The ML Hackathon 2025 is an exhilarating 48-hour competition for machine learning enthusiasts, developers, and data scientists to build innovative solutions. Compete for prizes, showcase your skills, and collaborate with peers in this high-energy event driving the future of AI.

Benefits:

  • Apply ML skills to real-world problems
  • Compete for prizes and recognition
  • Collaborate with top ML talent
