Deep Learning Bootcamp 2025

Event Details

Date: March 29-30, 2025

Location: AI Lab B

Description: The Deep Learning Bootcamp 2025 is an intensive two-day program designed to immerse participants in the world of deep neural networks. Led by AI experts, this hands-on bootcamp covers foundational concepts, advanced techniques, and practical applications using frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch. From image recognition to natural language processing, attendees will build and train models, gaining the skills to solve complex problems in this rapidly evolving field.

Benefits:

  • Master deep learning with expert instruction
  • Build real-world AI models hands-on
  • Advance your career in artificial intelligence
