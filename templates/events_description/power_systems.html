Power Systems Summit 2025

Event Details

Date: March 23, 2025

Location: Energy Research Center

Description: The Power Systems Summit 2025 is an insightful event that explores the future of electrical power systems. This summit brings together industry experts, engineers, and researchers to discuss advancements in power generation, smart grids, renewable energy integration, and power transmission. Learn about cutting-edge technologies shaping the energy sector and network with key stakeholders.

Key Topics:

  • Renewable energy and grid integration
  • Smart grids and power distribution
  • Advancements in power transmission
  • Energy storage solutions
