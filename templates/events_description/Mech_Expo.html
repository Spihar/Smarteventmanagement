Mechanical Expo 2025

Event Details

Date: March 29-30, 2025

Location: Engineering Pavilion

Description: The Mechanical Expo 2025 is a premier showcase of innovation in mechanical engineering, bringing together industry professionals, students, and technology enthusiasts. This two-day event features live demonstrations of advanced machinery, robotics, and mechanical systems, alongside expert-led talks on emerging trends like automation and sustainable design. Attendees can explore cutting-edge tools, network with leaders, and discover solutions driving the future of mechanical engineering.

Benefits:

  • Experience live mechanical tech demos
  • Connect with industry innovators
  • Learn about automation and sustainability
