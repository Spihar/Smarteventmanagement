AutoCAD Workshop 2025

Event Details

Date: March 29, 2025

Location: Design Studio B

Description: The AutoCAD Workshop 2025 is an immersive hands-on session designed to elevate your skills in computer-aided design. Led by certified AutoCAD experts, this workshop covers everything from foundational drafting techniques to advanced 3D modeling, tailored for architects, engineers, and designers. Participants will work on real-world projects, mastering precision tools and workflows to bring their ideas to life with industry-standard software.

Benefits:

  • Learn AutoCAD from certified professionals
  • Gain hands-on experience with real projects
  • Enhance your design and drafting skills
