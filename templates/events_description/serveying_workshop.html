Surveying Workshop 2025

Event Details

Date: March 23, 2025

Location: Civil Engineering Lab

Description: The Surveying Workshop 2025 is a hands-on training event designed for students, professionals, and enthusiasts in the field of land surveying. This workshop will cover essential techniques, modern surveying equipment, and GIS applications used in urban planning, construction, and environmental studies.

Key Learning Areas:

  • Hands-on training with total stations & GPS
  • Understanding land measurement techniques
  • Introduction to GIS & mapping tools
  • Practical applications in real-world projects
