🌠 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) 🌠

🤖 What is TechXplore 2025?

TechXplore 2025 is a tech innovation fest featuring AI, cybersecurity, IoT, robotics, and hands-on workshops.

📅 How can I register for an event?

Go to the Events page, select your event, and complete the registration process.

📍 Where is the event happening?

TechXplore 2025 will be hosted at BITS Hyderabad. Venue details are available on the event page.

💰 Do I need to pay to participate?

Some events are free, while others have an entry fee. Check the specific event page for details.

💻 Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes! You can register for multiple events as long as the schedules don’t overlap.

🎟️ How do I get my event pass?

After registration, you’ll receive a digital pass via email, which you must show at the venue for entry.

🏆 Are there prizes for competitions?

Yes! Winners get exciting cash prizes, certificates, and networking opportunities with top companies.

📞 Who do I contact for support?

For any queries, visit the Contact Support page or email us at interstellar@techxplore2025.com.
